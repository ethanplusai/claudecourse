# Cold Outreach Templates

Three-message sequences for email and LinkedIn. Customize with your offer and audience.

---

## Email Sequence

### Email 1: Lead with value

**Subject:** Free [tool/training/resource] for [AUDIENCE]

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST_NAME],

We built a free [DESCRIBE YOUR PLATFORM IN ONE LINE — e.g. "hiring platform that helps employers write better job ads and manage applicants"].

It's completely free. No catch. No sales pitch on the other side.

We're sharing it with [AUDIENCE — e.g. "growing companies"] who might find it useful: [LINK]

Would love your feedback if you check it out.

[YOUR NAME]

---

### Email 2: Add context (send 3 days later if no reply)

**Subject:** Re: Free [tool/training/resource] for [AUDIENCE]

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST_NAME],

Quick follow-up on the [PLATFORM NAME] I mentioned.

We built it because [ONE SENTENCE EXPLAINING WHY — e.g. "most employers waste hours writing job ads that don't attract the right candidates"].

[NUMBER] [AUDIENCE MEMBERS] are already using it. Figured it might be useful for [THEIR COMPANY/SITUATION]: [LINK]

No strings attached.

[YOUR NAME]

---

### Email 3: Light follow-up (send 5 days later if no reply)

**Subject:** Last one from me

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST_NAME],

Last follow-up on this. Built a free [PLATFORM DESCRIPTION] here: [LINK]

If the timing isn't right, no worries at all. Just didn't want you to miss it if it's relevant.

[YOUR NAME]

---

## LinkedIn Sequence

### Message 1: Connection request note

[FIRST_NAME] — saw you're [SOMETHING SPECIFIC ABOUT THEM]. We built a free [PLATFORM DESCRIPTION] that might be useful. Happy to share if you're interested.

### Message 2: After connection accepted

Hey [FIRST_NAME], thanks for connecting.

Here's the [PLATFORM NAME] I mentioned: [LINK]

It's free — built it to help [AUDIENCE] with [PROBLEM]. Let me know if you have any questions.

### Message 3: Follow-up (1 week later if no response)

Hey [FIRST_NAME] — any chance you checked out [PLATFORM NAME]? Would love to hear what you think if you get a chance: [LINK]

---

## Tips

- **Never pitch your paid service in cold outreach.** Only offer the free platform.
- **Personalize the first line.** Reference something specific about them or their company.
- **Keep it short.** Under 100 words per email. Under 50 words per LinkedIn message.
- **Use Claude Code to personalize at scale:** Feed it a list of prospects with context and ask it to write custom first lines for each.
