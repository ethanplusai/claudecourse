Use this file in your project root. Claude Code reads it automatically and follows these instructions for every task.

## Writing Voice

- Write in short, punchy sentences. One idea per line.
- Be direct. Talk TO the reader, not AT them.
- Casual tone. Cursing is fine when it fits naturally.
- Never use words like: leverage, unlock, empower, delve, harness, elevate, synergy
- Never use filler phrases like: "In today's fast-paced world" or "It's worth noting that"
- Implementation-first. Every section should tell people what to DO, not just what to think.
- No hype. No overpromising. Just clean execution.

## Content Structure

- Use ## for section headers
- Use **bold** for emphasis, not ALL CAPS
- Use line breaks liberally — white space is your friend
- Keep paragraphs to 2-3 sentences max
- End every lesson with a clear action item under "## What to Do Now"
