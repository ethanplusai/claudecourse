# Email Nurture Sequence Template

Use these templates as starting points. Feed them to Claude Code along with your audience context to customize.

---

## Email 1: Welcome (sent immediately after signup)

**Subject:** You're in, [FIRST_NAME]. Let's build.

**Body:**

[FIRST_NAME],

Welcome to [YOUR PLATFORM NAME].

You just took the first step toward [OUTCOME YOUR AUDIENCE WANTS].

Here's what's happening right now: your account is set up, your first [module/lesson/tool] is ready, and the system you're about to [learn/use] is already working.

Start here: [LINK TO FIRST LESSON]

Talk soon,
[YOUR NAME]

P.S. — Pay attention to the emails you receive from us over the next few days. They change based on what you do inside the platform. That's behavior-based nurture — and it's exactly what [you'll learn to build / makes this system work].

---

## Email 2: Teach (sent after first lesson completion)

**Subject:** Here's the one thing most [AUDIENCE] get wrong

**Body:**

[FIRST_NAME],

Most [AUDIENCE — e.g. "agency owners"] think [COMMON MISCONCEPTION].

The truth is [REFRAME].

That's why [YOUR PLATFORM] is built around [YOUR APPROACH] instead of [THE OLD WAY].

Your next lesson covers this in detail: [LINK]

---

## Email 3: Reframe (sent after 2-3 lessons)

**Subject:** Why [COMMON APPROACH] doesn't work anymore

**Body:**

[FIRST_NAME],

You've probably tried [THING THEY'VE TRIED]. Maybe it worked for a minute. Maybe it didn't work at all.

Here's why: [EXPLANATION].

What works instead: [YOUR METHOD].

Lesson [X] breaks this down step by step: [LINK]

---

## Email 4: Proof (sent at ~50% completion)

**Subject:** How [EXAMPLE PERSON/COMPANY] did it

**Body:**

[FIRST_NAME],

[BRIEF CASE STUDY OR EXAMPLE — can be hypothetical if you don't have real ones yet].
